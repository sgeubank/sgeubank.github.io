Memos
The entries related to FQCG in the "Name and Subject Index to the General Correspondence of the War Plans Division". The general correspondence and the index are part of Records of the War Department General and Special Staffs, Record Group 165. From the "file number" field in each entry, you can (usually) get the microfilm reel, which may or may not have been digitized yet. If not digitized, the reel should be available to the public at the Archives in Maryland.
  • Overcoats. Oct - Nov, 1917
  • No funds for depot. Nov, 1917
  • Field artillery materiel. Dec 1, 1917
  • Related to Houston race riot. Feb - May, 1918
  • Liaison with State Dept. March, 1918
  • Sent with Stimson to NYC to investigate loyalty of expeditionary force June, 1917
  • Testing torpedos. Aug, 1918
  • Purchasing. March, 1919
  • Form committee. May, 1919
